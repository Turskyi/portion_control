Portion Control App

Portion Control is a simple app that helps you manage your food portions and track your daily intake. Instead of counting calories, you log meals in grams and adjust your portions based on weight trends.

How Portion Control Works

Track what you eat, monitor your weight, and gradually find the right portion sizes for your body. This approach focuses on consistency and long-term habits rather than strict calorie tracking.

Key Features

  • Track food portions in grams
  • Monitor body weight trends
  • Simple and distraction-free interface
  • No calorie counting required

Portion Control for Weight Management

Portion control is a proven method to support healthy weight management. By adjusting how much you eat instead of what you eat, you can build sustainable habits over time.
